> answer one question: will the operation keep running when it matters?**
> EMaaS keeps the power on and honestly sized. Signal Engine sees the
> regulatory and threat signals before they become emergencies. M365 Turnkey
> gives the owner a visible operating route so execution stops living in
> people's heads. One discipline — make the invisible work visible — applied
> to energy, risk, and operations. And SG runs itself on every one of these
> before selling any of them: the app sized real jobs, the engine runs 4x/day
> on our own BD, and our own company runs on the M365 build we deploy.

short cycle); EMaaS advisory = mid-cycle, high credibility (free app funnel);
Signal Engine = longest cycle, largest tickets (partner-delivered). Cash flow
narrative: quick wins fund patient pipelines.
